How It Works

Just speak.
Knot does the rest.

01
📷

Pick a photo

Scan a physical print or choose from your iPad. No technical steps.

02
🎙️

Tell the story

Just press record and speak. Knot listens, captures your voice and emotion.

03
✨

Watch it come alive

AI animates the photo — a smile, a blink — matched to the feeling in your words.

04
🔗

Share the memory

Send a link. Your family experiences grandma's warmth — not just her picture.
